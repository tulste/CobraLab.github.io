title: A new way to detect Alzheimer's Disease

Check out the video below where Christine Tardif and Mallar Chakravarty talk to Laura Casella from Global News Montreal about recent research from our group where a link between MRI-based hippocampal volume estimates and biomarkers have been found in patients years before cognitive decline. For more information about this research, click the link to read the published paper [Tardif et al. (2017)](http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/hbm.23897/full)
